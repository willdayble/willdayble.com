All about Will

Geek turned teacher

Will’s background is digital and entrepreneurial, having founded multiple businesses across tech, social enterprise, hospitality, teaching, and impact measurement.

In high school, he was presented with a ‘rebel without a cause’ award, after barely scraping through VCE.

These days, Will delivers keynotes and masterclasses on disruption, innovation and digital strategy with Fortune 500s, the social sector and the startup world. He sits on a few boards and reads lots of science fiction.

Will has written, delivered and examined full university courses, and speaks at various education, digital and entrepreneurial events.

He’s passionate about measuring impact, breaking dumb systems, diversity within digital and supporting the Australian social startup economy.

In his spare time he doesn’t have any.

Will co-founded these things:

1
Squareweave, a web and mobile dev company making the world better with technology;
2
Grace, a cafe in Fitzroy;
3
The Cowalition, a social enterprise startup creating impact with small-plot farmers in Cambodia;
4
The Fitzroy Academy of Getting Shit Done, where we teach people to build things that don’t exist yet.
