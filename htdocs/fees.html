No-so-fine print

Professional services fees are provided as a guide for budgeting purposes and may vary according to your outcome goals. Amounts quoted do not allow for personal expenses directly related to the activity (such as transportation and accommodation) and should be added where appropriate.

Prices quoted in Australian Dollars. Special rates may apply for education and non-profit organisations, and for multiple/bundled bookings.

International engagements can sometimes attract a higher fee.

* For multi-day bookings, Will is also available to serve as an event guide at favourable rates.

** Event Guide is like having an MC with a built in Keynote. I’ll guide event introductions, speaking, thanks to sponsors, Q+A and then wrap it all up with a keynote touching on key themes of the day.
